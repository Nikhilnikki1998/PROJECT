The Founders of the Nvidia

         

Jen-Hsun "Jensen" Huang is a Taiwanese-American billionaire
businessman and electrical engineer. He co-founded the
graphics-processor company Nvidia in 1993 and has been
its president and chief executive officer since inception. Huang
graduated from Oregon State University before moving to
California

Raised in the Oakhurst section

Township High School in 1976.
